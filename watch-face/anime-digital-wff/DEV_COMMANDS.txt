SWAP BACKGROUND IMAGE (change active face)
------------------------------------------
Edit: app\src\main\res\raw\watchface.xml
Change: <Image resource="face_01_sunset_boy" />
To any of:
  face_01_sunset_boy
  face_02_twilight_girl
  face_03_blue_lightning
  face_04_red_flame
  face_05_green_forest
  face_06_purple_curse
  face_07_gold_sky
  face_08_cyber_neon
Then rebuild and run.


ADD A NEW FACE IMAGE
--------------------
1. Drop new PNG into: app\src\main\res\drawable-nodpi\
2. Name it: face_09_yourname.png
3. Reference it in watchface.xml: <Image resource="face_09_yourname" />
